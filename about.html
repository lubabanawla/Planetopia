Our project mission...

During Ascend, our prompt told us to design a planet and asked "what would be a planet you would want to visit?" After brainstorming, we realized there's no right answer to this question. Everyone has their own utopia and our project lives up to that. Though we faced a turbulent journey filled with red underlined code, errors, and misunderstandings of our direction, we were able to pull something off, something we like to call "mission success." Embark on your own journey by clicking blast off, create your own planet and learn the background behind it taking a quick visit to your planet using your own personal tour guide!
